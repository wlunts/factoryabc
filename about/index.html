About this project

Purpose: to promote easy and secure business around the world through sharing practical factories and onsite typical findings.

Flag: I will continue this project with 1 ~ 2 articles each week until Dec 31th, 2024 at least, except Chinese holiday.

Inspection finding: most are typical issues which you better remind your factory before production.

Resource: enjoy yourself on all sharing: “Oversea” means factory has foreign trade department, “Domestic” = no, but do business via trader or broker.
